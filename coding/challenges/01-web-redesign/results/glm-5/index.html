About Us

Built by teams, for teams

SkillSync started as an internal tool born from frustration. Our founding team was tired of toggling between five different apps just to understand what everyone was working on.

What began as a simple task tracker evolved into a comprehensive workspace. We added docs, real-time updates, and smart integrations so teams could spend less time managing tools and more time doing meaningful work.

Today, over 10,000 teams across 38 countries rely on SkillSync to keep their projects on track. From fast-growing startups to established enterprises, our customers share one thing in common: they chose simplicity over complexity.
