Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Can we migrate data from another tool?

Yes. CSV imports are supported for projects, tasks, and team members. We also have one-click importers for Trello and Asana.

Does SkillSync include onboarding help?

Growth and Enterprise plans include guided onboarding sessions to help your team get set up correctly.

Is there an annual contract?

No contract is required for monthly billing. However, annual billing offers a 20% discount.
